Products & Services

Spanish Conjugator

I have not been given information on what this app is about and therefore, cannot describe it. So instead, I'm just going to explain how this product/service listing works.

Based on my limited knowledge of what you're trying to accomplish, you're going to need a place to display all of the apps that you release. That's what the “Products & Services” section of your site was built to do.

The Products & Services page will be a catalog of all the apps you have released. Every time you release an app, you will create a new entry on the Products & Services page. As you release more apps and add more entries to the Products & Services page, the catalog will grow exponentially.

The page that you're on now is an example of what an entry should look like. It will contain the title of the app which should be just above, along with a description of the app, which would be here where this explanation is written.

There should also be a slideshow on this page. The slideshow will be where all of the screenshots of the app will be, should you choose to add any. I put one of the screenshots in the space where the slideshow would be. Unfortunately, I’m unable to create the slideshow on this mock-up without Wordpress. You can see an example of a Wordpress slideshow here. Try to use your imagination on what it would look like on this page.

Below is a button that when clicked, will lead to the app page on the app store. When creating an entry on the Products & Services page, you will be asked to add a link to the product on the app store. Once you provide the requested information, the “Download on the App Store” button will be dynamically created. If you do not add the information, the button will not appear on the page. You’ll always have the option to add the information at a later date should you chose not to add it when asked.

There really isn’t much else to explain. I hope you understand what all of this means now. If you are still confused as to what this is, feel free to reach out to me. I’ll explain it to the best of my ability.
