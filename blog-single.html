A Subheading

What your reading is a blog post. To understand what a blog post is, you have to understand what a blog is. According to Wikipedia:

'A blog (a truncation of the expression "weblog") is a discussion or informational website published on the World Wide Web consisting of discrete, often informal diary-style text entries ("posts").'

Essentially, a blog is a regularly updated website containing information on any topic you chose to write about. Whether you chose to write about how your day is going or a tutorial is irrelevant. A blog can be about anything you want it to be about. The individual entries on your blog are called “blog post”. That's basically what this thing that your reading is.
