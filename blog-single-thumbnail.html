This is an example of what your blog post will look like with a “featured image”. You're meant to read this post second. If your unsure of what a blog post is, please read the entry titled “Sample blog post” first.

In Wordpress, a Featured Image (also known as a post thumbnail) is an image used to represent a post. Most people just call them thumbnails. Ideally, once your site is completed you’ll be able to upload thumbnails for your post. This is how your blog post should appear when it is given a thumbnail.
