New feature

May 20, 2018 by Warren McCoy Jr

The other pages should explain what blog posts are and what they can be. In this section, I'll attempt to explain what this specific page is.

The page that your on now is the main blog page. Here, you'll be able to access all of your individual blog posts, which will all have there own pages that will be created dynamically.

The section to the side (or the bottom before the footer if your viewing on mobile) titled "Archives" is in a special section of the blog called the sidebar.

The sidebar is generally meant to hold extra information about the blog, but it can contain anything you want it to contain. Currently, it displays an example of a monthly archive, which is all of your blog posts organized by month. Previously, there was also another sidebar that displayed a list of your social media links but that's been removed. I think its a better idea to display all of your social media links in the footer of your site

The links in the sidebar on this mock-up are not functional. The sidebar is only there to demonstrate what it could look like.

Have you noticed how the other 2 post above only have a small portion of the post displayed on this page while this one seems to have the entire post displayed. That's because WordPress allows you to create excerpts to display on the main blog page where the post is listed. This is an example of what the post would appear like without specifying an excerpt point. It was done on purpose to contrast the examples that do have excerpts.

That sums up the blog page. Please proceed to the Products page if you haven't already done so to see how product/service listings will work.
